Block Walls

Structural integrity, security, and style for your property's foundation.

The Foundation of a Great Design

Block walls are the unsung heroes of landscape design. They do the heavy lifting—literally. In San Diego County's hilly terrain, a properly engineered retaining wall is often the most critical part of a pool installation.

Beyond their structural importance, block walls also define spaces, provide privacy, and add beautiful, multi-level dimensions to your backyard. We build all our walls to rigorous codes for safety and longevity.

  • Structural Support: Engineer-approved retaining walls to create level, usable land.
  • Privacy & Security: Solid perimeter walls for a secure and secluded oasis.
  • Aesthetic Finishes: Finished with stucco or stone veneer to perfectly match your home.

Our Masonry Services

Retaining Walls

Essential for slopes, these engineered walls are built with deep footings and proper drainage to safely hold back soil, creating the level pad needed for your pool and patio.

Garden & Privacy Walls

Define your property line and create a private, intimate setting. These walls are perfect for enclosing your backyard and reducing noise.

Seat Walls & Planters

We integrate low, 18-inch-high seat walls around patios and fire pits for built-in, permanent seating. We also construct raised planter walls to add beautiful landscaping.

Finished for a Custom Look

A grey cinder block wall is just the start. The magic is in the finish. We ensure that every wall we build is a beautiful feature, not just a utility.

Our masons apply finishes that perfectly match your pool, patio, and home's exterior.

  • Smooth Stucco: A clean, modern look. We can apply a smooth or textured stucco finish and paint it to match your home's exterior color.
  • Stone Veneer: We apply high-quality stacked stone or ledger stone veneers that match the stone used on your spa, pool, or BBQ island.
  • Wall Caps: The final touch. We finish the top of the wall with pre-cast concrete caps or paver coping to match your pool deck.
