Pool Solar Systems

Extend your swim season and heat your pool for free using the San Diego sun.

Swim Warmer, Swim Longer

Why let your swim season be limited to just a few short months? In San Diego, the sun provides a powerful, free, and eco-friendly resource to heat your pool. A pool solar system is the most cost-effective and environmentally friendly way to warm your pool water.

We install high-efficiency solar panel systems that can raise your pool's temperature by 10-15 degrees, effectively doubling your comfortable swim season without the monthly sticker shock of a gas or electric heater.

  • Zero Operating Costs: Heats your pool using free energy from the sun.
  • Eco-Friendly: No emissions, no fossil fuels. Just clean, green energy.
  • Low Maintenance: Systems are durable and designed to last 15-20 years with minimal upkeep.

How It Works

Our solar heating system is simple, efficient, and works with your existing pool pump.

1. Pump

Your pool pump sends cold water from the pool up to the solar collectors on your roof.

2. Heat

The sun heats the water as it passes through the many small tubes inside the solar collectors.

3. Return

The now-warm water flows back down from the roof and returns to your pool.

4. Swim!

This simple loop repeats all day, gradually raising your pool to a comfortable temperature.

A Smarter Investment

While gas or electric heat pumps can heat a pool faster, they come with high installation costs and even higher monthly utility bills.

A solar system is a one-time investment that typically pays for itself in just 2-3 years from the money you save on gas or electricity.

  • Gas Heaters: Very expensive to run. Monthly bills can be hundreds of dollars. Not environmentally friendly.
  • Electric Heat Pumps: More efficient than gas, but still add a significant cost to your electric bill and rely on the grid.
  • Solar Heaters: No monthly operating costs. Works anytime the sun is shining. The system pays for itself.
