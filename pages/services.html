Our Services

From concept to completion, we offer a comprehensive suite of services to build your perfect backyard oasis.

1. Custom Pool Installation

Your dream pool, built from the ground up. We specialize in high-quality gunite (shotcrete) and fiberglass pools, designed to fit your unique space and lifestyle. Our process ensures meticulous attention to detail, from 3D design and excavation to plumbing and the final finish.

We handle everything, including modern features like smart controls, energy-efficient equipment, and stunning water features like waterfalls or deck jets.

Learn More

2. Pavers & Decking

The right pool deck frames your oasis and connects it to your home. We install a wide variety of high-quality pavers, including elegant natural stone, cool-touch travertine, and durable interlocking concrete pavers.

Our expert installation ensures a durable, non-slip, and beautiful surface that lasts for decades. We also offer sealing and maintenance to keep your deck looking new.

Learn More

3. Pool Remodeling

Is your existing pool looking outdated or showing signs of wear? We can transform it into a modern masterpiece. Our remodeling services include new plaster or pebble finishes, waterline tile replacement, and coping repair.

We can also add new features like a spa, baja shelf, or color-changing LED lights, and upgrade you to an energy-efficient variable-speed pump or saltwater system.

Learn More

4. BBQs & Fire Pits

Extend your living space and entertain year-round with a custom-built BBQ island, outdoor kitchen, or a cozy fire pit. We construct beautiful, functional hardscape features that become the focal point for gatherings.

We handle all integration, including running gas lines and electrical, to create a seamless and professional outdoor kitchen setup.

Learn More

5. Exterior Design

A pool is just one part of the perfect backyard. Our exterior design services tie everything together. We plan and install landscape lighting, artificial turf, and water-wise plant selections.

We create a "total environment" that flows beautifully from your home to your pool, considering views, shade, and ambiance.

Learn More

6. Block Walls

Define your space with structural and decorative block walls. We build everything from engineered retaining walls, essential for San Diego's topography, to beautiful privacy walls and planters.

Our team ensures every wall is built to code with proper footings and rebar, and we can finish them with stucco, stone veneer, or paint to perfectly match your home.

Learn More

7. Pool Solar System

Enjoy a warmer pool for more of the year without the high gas bills. We install high-efficiency pool solar heating systems that use the free power of the sun to heat your pool.

It's an eco-friendly, low-maintenance solution that significantly extends your swim season, maximizing the investment you've made in your backyard.

Learn More
