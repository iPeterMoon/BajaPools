Pavers & Pool Decking

The perfect foundation for your backyard oasis, combining beauty, safety, and durability.

The Foundation of Your Oasis

A pool deck is more than just a surface; it's the stage that frames your pool and connects your home to your new outdoor living space. At Baja Pools, we create stunning, durable, and non-slip decks using the highest quality pavers.

Our expert installation ensures a perfectly level, stable, and long-lasting foundation that resists the harsh San Diego sun and heavy foot traffic. We prepare a compacted base and set each paver with precision for a flawless finish.

  • Safe & Non-Slip: Textured surfaces for maximum safety around wet areas.
  • Durable & Long-Lasting: Resistant to cracking, fading, and staining.
  • Low Maintenance: Easy to clean and individual pavers can be replaced if needed.

Our Decking Materials

Interlocking Pavers

The industry standard for durability and value. Available in a vast array of shapes, colors, and textures to mimic stone or create modern patterns.

Natural Stone

For a luxury look, we install Travertine and Flagstone. Travertine stays cool to the touch, making it a perfect choice for San Diego's sunny climate.

Porcelain Pavers

A modern, high-tech option that offers a sleek, uniform look with incredible stain resistance and durability. Ideal for clean, contemporary designs.

Design & Features

The design is in the details. Our team helps you select the perfect paver and pattern—from classic running bond to intricate herringbone—to complement your home's architecture.

We go beyond the flat surfaces to build a complete, integrated hardscape.

  • Custom Coping: The "bullnose" or cantilevered edge of your pool, perfectly matched to your deck.
  • Steps & Raised Patios: We build beautiful, safe steps and raised patio areas for multi-level backyards.
  • Integrated Lighting: Add subtle hardscape lighting for ambiance and safety at night.
