Exterior Design

A cohesive vision that unites your pool, patio, and landscape into a single, stunning space.

A Holistic Design Approach

A world-class backyard is more than just a pool. It's a complete, integrated environment where every element works in harmony. Our exterior design service focuses on creating that cohesive vision for your entire San Diego property.

We don't just build features; we design experiences. We consider how you'll move through the space, where you'll entertain, and how the landscape will complement the architecture of your home.

  • Seamless Integration: Connecting your home, pool, patio, and garden.
  • Maximize Usability: Creating functional zones for dining, relaxing, and playing.
  • Unified Aesthetics: Ensuring materials, colors, and styles are consistent.

Elements of Our Designs

Hardscaping

The foundation. This includes your pool deck, patios, walkways, retaining walls, and any stone or paver surfaces.

Softscaping

The living elements. We help select and place drought-tolerant plants, trees, shrubs, and turf to add color, texture, and privacy.

Landscape Lighting

Essential for ambiance and safety. We design low-voltage lighting systems that highlight trees, pathways, and water features.

Shade Structures

We can design and build pergolas, patio covers, and other shade structures to create comfortable, usable space out of the sun.

See It Before We Build It

The most important part of our design process is our advanced 3D modeling. We don't just ask you to imagine your new backyard—we show it to you.

Our designers will create a photorealistic 3D render of your entire property, allowing you to take a virtual tour. You can see how the morning sun hits the water, how the landscape lighting will look at night, and make changes to materials or layouts with confidence.

  • Make Confident Decisions: Change paver colors or plant sizes virtually.
  • Ensure Perfect Flow: Verify that pathways are wide enough and seating areas feel right.
  • A Clear Blueprint: The final 3D model becomes the exact plan our construction team follows.
