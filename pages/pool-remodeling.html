Pool Remodeling

Transform your outdated pool into the modern oasis you've always dreamed of.

Why Remodel Your Pool?

Is your pool showing its age? A remodel can do more than just update the look—it can improve safety, increase energy efficiency, and add valuable new features for your family to enjoy.

Whether your plaster is stained, your tile is cracked, or you're simply ready for a change, our team can breathe new life into your backyard. We handle everything from simple surface upgrades to complete structural transformations.

  • Update Aesthetics: Replace dated tile and worn-out surfaces.
  • Boost Efficiency: Upgrade to variable-speed pumps and LED lighting.
  • Add New Features: Install a spa, baja shelf, or water features.

Our Remodeling Services

Resurfacing

The single biggest change. We chip out the old surface and apply a new, beautiful finish like modern pebble, quartz, or classic plaster.

Tile & Coping

Update your pool's "frame" with modern waterline tile and safe, stylish new coping around the edge.

Equipment Upgrades

Save money and hassle by upgrading to a variable-speed pump, saltwater system, or smart controls you can manage from your phone.

Feature Additions

Add the features you've always wanted, from a relaxing baja shelf or integrated spa to elegant waterfalls and deck jets.

Add Modern Features

A remodel is the perfect time to add features that completely change how you use your pool. We can structurally modify your existing pool shell to include:

  • Baja Shelves: A large, shallow shelf (6-12 inches deep) perfect for lounge chairs and kids.
  • Integrated Spas: Add a custom gunite spa that overflows into your pool, usable year-round.
  • Water & Fire Features: From sheer descent waterfalls to rock grottos and fire bowls.
  • LED Lighting: Replace your old single bulb with vibrant, color-changing LED lights.
