Custom Pool Installation

Building your San Diego dream oasis from 3D design to first swim.

Baja Pools: The Gold Standard

At Baja Pools, we specialize in custom-built gunite (shotcrete) pools, the gold standard for durability and complete design freedom. Unlike pre-molded fiberglass pools, a gunite pool is built on-site, allowing us to create any shape, size, or depth you can imagine.

This method is ideal for San Diego's diverse landscapes, from hillside properties to compact backyards. The steel-rebar framework and pneumatic-applied concrete shell create a monolithic, rock-solid structure that will last for decades.

  • Limitless Customization: Curves, sharp angles, baja shelves, integrated spas.
  • Ultimate Durability: A steel-reinforced shell built to last.
  • Flexible Finishes: Choose from classic plaster, smooth pebble, or all-tile finishes.

Pools We Specialize In

Geometric & Modern

Clean lines, sharp angles, and features like perimeter overflows or knife-edge designs for a stunning, contemporary look.

Freeform & Natural

Soft curves and lagoon-style designs that blend seamlessly with your landscaping, often paired with rock waterfalls and beach entries.

Spas & Spools

Maximize small spaces with a "spool" (small pool) or add a fully integrated, custom-built spa for year-round relaxation.

Our Installation Process

We manage every step with precision and constant communication, turning your vision into a reality.

1

Envision

Begin with an on-site consultation and 3D design. We'll strategize to offer a solution tailored to your desires, ensuring it aligns with your timeline and budget.

2

Plan

We finalize materials, secure all necessary city and county permits, and set a precise project timeline so you know exactly what's happening and when.

3

Construct

From excavation and rebar to gunite, plumbing, and electrical, our in-house crews handle every step with expert craftsmanship and quality checks.

4

Thrive

After the final finish is applied and the water is balanced, we provide a "Pool School" to teach you how to use your new equipment. It's time to enjoy!
